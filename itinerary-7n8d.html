7 Nights / 8 Days Kashmir Tour

Deep dive into Kashmir’s valleys, mountains, and culture.

Itinerary Overview

Day 1 – Srinagar Arrival

Check-in at Dal Lake houseboat, evening Shikara ride and sightseeing of local bazaars.

Day 2 – Srinagar & Mughal Gardens

Visit Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, explore floating vegetable markets, evening leisure on houseboat.

Day 3 – Gulmarg

Gondola ride, meadow walk, optional skiing or snow activities, sunset photography.

Day 4 – Pahalgam

Visit Lidder River, Baisaran meadow, horse rides, riverside picnic, evening return to Srinagar.

Day 5 – Sonmarg

Explore glaciers, short treks, riverside picnic, photography of Himalayan landscapes.

Day 6 – Yusmarg

Hiking in pine forests, visit meadows, enjoy serene landscapes, return to Srinagar.

Day 7 – Gurez Valley

Day trip to Gurez Valley, meet locals, explore traditional villages, experience untouched nature.

Day 8 – Departure

Morning leisure, final shopping, and airport drop.
