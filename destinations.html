Destinations Across Kashmir

Kashmir is a land of contrasts — serene lakes, dramatic mountains, lush valleys, and timeless villages. Each destination carries its own rhythm, story, and charm. At JustTheRoutes, we help you explore Kashmir at a pace that lets you truly feel it.

Srinagar

The heart of Kashmir, Srinagar is where tradition meets tranquility. Famous for Dal Lake, Mughal gardens, and houseboats, the city offers a gentle introduction to Kashmiri culture, cuisine, and hospitality.

Gulmarg

A paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers, Gulmarg is known for its snow-covered slopes, Gondola rides, and breathtaking alpine views. In winter, it transforms into one of India’s top skiing destinations.

Pahalgam

Nestled along the Lidder River, Pahalgam is perfect for travelers seeking peace and scenic beauty. Meadows, pine forests, and river walks make it a favorite for families and honeymooners alike.

Sonmarg

Often called the “Meadow of Gold,” Sonmarg is the gateway to glaciers, high-altitude treks, and some of Kashmir’s most dramatic landscapes.

Every destination we offer is carefully selected for safety, comfort, and authentic experiences — ensuring your journey through Kashmir is both memorable and worry-free.
