Discover Kashmir’s Most Beautiful Destinations

Every valley, lake, and meadow tells a story.

Srinagar

The heart of Kashmir, Srinagar enchants travelers with Dal Lake, Mughal gardens, and colorful houseboats. Its bustling bazaars and vibrant culture offer an authentic local experience, where every corner has a story to tell.

Gulmarg

Known as the “Meadow of Flowers,” Gulmarg is a paradise for adventure and nature lovers. Skiing in winter, gondola rides, and wildflower meadows in summer make it a year-round destination.

Pahalgam

Nestled along the Lidder River, Pahalgam offers serene landscapes and leisurely walks through pine forests. Ideal for families and honeymooners, it combines adventure with peace and calm.

Sonmarg

Often called the “Meadow of Gold,” Sonmarg dazzles visitors with glaciers, alpine streams, and high-altitude treks. Its pristine beauty is perfect for photographers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Doodhpathri

A hidden gem of Kashmir, Doodhpathri is famous for its rolling meadows, crystal streams, and quiet charm. It’s perfect for travelers seeking solitude amidst nature.

Gurez Valley

Remote and breathtaking, Gurez Valley offers untouched landscapes, traditional villages, and a chance to experience Kashmiri culture away from tourist crowds. A journey here feels timeless.

Yusmarg

Known for lush green meadows and panoramic views of snow-capped peaks, Yusmarg is a paradise for picnics, short treks, and nature walks. Its tranquility is unmatched.
