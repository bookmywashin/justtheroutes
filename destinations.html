Srinagar

The heart of Kashmir, known for Dal Lake, Mughal gardens, and rich culture.

Srinagar blends centuries of history with natural beauty. From peaceful Shikara rides on Dal Lake to vibrant local markets and Mughal-era gardens, the city offers an immersive introduction to Kashmir. Staying on a traditional houseboat and enjoying authentic Kashmiri cuisine makes Srinagar an experience that stays with you long after you leave.
