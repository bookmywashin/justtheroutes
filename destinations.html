Kashmir Destinations

Every valley tells a story

Srinagar

The heart of Kashmir, known for Dal Lake, Mughal gardens, and rich culture.

Srinagar blends centuries of history with natural beauty. From peaceful Shikara rides on Dal Lake to vibrant local markets and Mughal-era gardens, the city offers an immersive introduction to Kashmir. Staying on a traditional houseboat and enjoying authentic Kashmiri cuisine makes Srinagar an experience that stays with you long after you leave.

Gulmarg

Alpine meadows and adventure experiences throughout the year.

Gulmarg is famous for its powdery snow in winter and lush green meadows in summer. The Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world, offers breathtaking panoramic views. Whether skiing, trekking, or simply enjoying the mountain air, Gulmarg delivers unforgettable moments.

Read more

Pahalgam

A peaceful riverside town surrounded by forests and meadows.

Pahalgam is ideal for travelers seeking calm and natural beauty. The Lidder River flows gently through the town, while nearby meadows like Baisaran offer stunning views. Horse rides, riverside walks, and scenic drives make Pahalgam a favorite among families and honeymooners.

Sonmarg

The Meadow of Gold, surrounded by glaciers and high peaks.

Sonmarg offers raw, dramatic landscapes with towering mountains and flowing streams. It serves as a gateway to Himalayan treks and glaciers. Less commercialized than other destinations, Sonmarg is perfect for nature lovers and photographers seeking untouched beauty.

Gurez Valley

A remote valley rich in culture and untouched landscapes.

Gurez Valley offers an authentic glimpse into traditional Kashmiri life. Nestled near the Line of Control, the region remains largely unexplored. Wooden homes, friendly locals, and dramatic mountain views make Gurez a destination for travelers seeking authenticity and solitude.

Yusmarg

Wide open meadows and quiet trails away from the crowds.

Yusmarg is perfect for travelers who prefer peaceful landscapes over busy tourist hubs. Surrounded by pine forests and mountain views, it offers excellent opportunities for short hikes, picnics, and mindful travel. Yusmarg captures Kashmir in its most tranquil form.
