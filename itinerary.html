Detailed Kashmir Itineraries

Our itineraries are designed to give you a smooth, well-paced experience of Kashmir — balancing travel time, sightseeing, and rest.

Day 1 – Arrival in Srinagar

Arrival at Srinagar airport, transfer to hotel or houseboat. Evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake followed by a traditional Kashmiri dinner.

Day 2 – Srinagar to Gulmarg

Scenic drive to Gulmarg. Enjoy Gondola ride, snow activities (seasonal), and leisure walks through alpine meadows.

Day 3 – Gulmarg to Pahalgam

Travel through picturesque landscapes to Pahalgam. Explore Lidder River, local markets, and surrounding valleys.

Day 4 – Sonmarg Excursion

Day trip to Sonmarg, known for glaciers and high-altitude beauty. Return to Srinagar by evening.

Each itinerary can be adjusted based on weather, preferences, and travel pace.
