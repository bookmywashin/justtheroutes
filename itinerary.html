Your Kashmir Journey, Day by Day

Thoughtfully planned routes. Balanced pace. Authentic experiences.

Day 1 – Arrival in Srinagar

Upon arrival at Srinagar Airport, you are welcomed by our local representative and transferred to your hotel or traditional houseboat. The rest of the day is kept light to help you settle in comfortably.

In the evening, enjoy a serene Shikara ride on Dal Lake, gliding past floating gardens and wooden houseboats as the sun sets behind the mountains. This gentle introduction sets the tone for your Kashmir journey.

Day 2 – Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Today is dedicated to exploring Srinagar’s cultural and historical landmarks. Visit the Mughal Gardens — Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh — known for their terraced lawns, fountains, and breathtaking views.

Later, explore the old city markets where traditional Kashmiri handicrafts, dry fruits, and spices offer a glimpse into local life. The evening is free for leisure or optional shopping.

Day 3 – Excursion to Gulmarg

After breakfast, proceed towards Gulmarg, Kashmir’s premier hill resort. Surrounded by alpine forests and open meadows, Gulmarg is famous for its year-round beauty.

Experience the Gulmarg Gondola (cable car), one of the highest in the world, offering panoramic views of snow-clad peaks. Depending on the season, enjoy snow activities, meadow walks, or simply relax amidst nature.

Day 4 – Srinagar to Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam, a scenic town nestled along the Lidder River. En route, visit saffron fields and the ancient Awantipora ruins, adding historical depth to your journey.

Upon arrival, take a peaceful riverside walk or relax at your hotel. Pahalgam’s calm atmosphere makes it a favorite for families and honeymooners.

Day 5 – Pahalgam Exploration

Spend the day exploring Pahalgam’s surrounding meadows. Optional excursions include Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, or Baisaran, often called “Mini Switzerland.”

Horse rides, photography, and relaxed sightseeing define the day. The pace remains flexible, allowing you to enjoy Kashmir without rushing.

Day 6 – Pahalgam to Srinagar

After breakfast, return to Srinagar through scenic mountain roads. The drive itself is part of the experience, offering beautiful countryside views.

In the evening, enjoy leisure time for shopping or exploring local cafés. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 7 – Departure

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Srinagar Airport for your return journey, carrying memories of a thoughtfully curated Kashmir experience.
