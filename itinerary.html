5 Nights / 6 Days Kashmir Delight

Day 1 – Srinagar Arrival

Arrive in Srinagar, transfer to hotel or houseboat. Enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake and explore local markets. Evening is free for leisure and tasting traditional Kashmiri cuisine.

Day 2 – Srinagar to Gulmarg

Drive to Gulmarg through scenic valleys. Experience Gondola rides, snow sports in winter, and meadows in summer. Evening return and local cultural experience.

Day 3 – Pahalgam Exploration

Travel to Pahalgam. Walk along the Lidder River, explore meadows and pine forests, visit local villages, and enjoy peaceful landscapes perfect for photography.

Day 4 – Sonmarg Excursion

Take a day trip to Sonmarg. Witness glaciers, streams, and mountain views. Enjoy trekking, local guides, and a picnic in the meadows before returning to Srinagar.

Day 5 – Local Srinagar Experiences

Relax in Srinagar, visit Mughal gardens, local handicraft markets, and enjoy Kashmiri cuisine. Evening leisure or optional cultural show.

Day 6 – Departure

Checkout and transfer to Srinagar Airport. Carry home memories of timeless journeys and scenic beauty.
