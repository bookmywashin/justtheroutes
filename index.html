Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to travel to Kashmir?
Yes. Tourism operates normally across Kashmir. We continuously monitor local conditions and plan routes accordingly to ensure a safe and smooth experience.
Do you customize tour packages?
Absolutely. Every itinerary can be adjusted based on duration, interests, budget, and season.
What is the best time to visit Kashmir?
March to October is ideal for sightseeing and nature. December to February is perfect for snow lovers and winter experiences.
Are airport transfers included?
Yes. All our packages include airport pickup and drop with verified local drivers.
Do you assist during the trip?
Yes. Our team stays in touch throughout your journey to ensure everything runs smoothly.
