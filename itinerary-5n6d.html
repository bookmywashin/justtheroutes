5 Nights / 6 Days Kashmir Tour

Extended exploration of Kashmir’s valleys and cultural experiences.

Itinerary Overview

Day 1 – Srinagar

Arrival, check-in at Dal Lake houseboat, evening Shikara ride.

Day 2 – Gulmarg

Gondola ride, explore meadows, optional skiing, mountain photography.

Day 3 – Pahalgam

Lidder River walk, Baisaran meadow, horse rides, riverside picnic.

Day 4 – Sonmarg

Glacier views, short treks, riverside picnic, photography.

Day 5 – Yusmarg

Forest trails, meadows, relaxed exploration, return to Srinagar.

Day 6 – Departure

Leisure morning, airport transfer.
