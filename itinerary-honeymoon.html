Honeymoon Special

Romantic journey through the heart of Kashmir.

Itinerary Overview

Day 1 – Arrival & Houseboat Stay

Check-in at a Dal Lake houseboat, romantic Shikara ride, sunset view.

Day 2 – Srinagar & Mughal Gardens

Visit Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and enjoy couple photography sessions.

Day 3 – Gulmarg

Gondola ride, snow activities, meadow walks, romantic picnic.

Day 4 – Pahalgam

Stroll along Lidder River, Baisaran meadow, horse rides, intimate valley walks.

Day 5 – Leisure & Departure

Relaxed morning, optional shopping, airport transfer.
