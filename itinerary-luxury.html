Luxury Kashmir Escape

Indulge in premium stays, private tours, and curated experiences.

Itinerary Overview

Day 1 – Arrival in Srinagar

Luxury houseboat check-in, private Shikara ride, evening fine dining.

Day 2 – Srinagar & Mughal Gardens

Private guided tours, photography sessions, leisure in premium resorts.

Day 3 – Gulmarg Adventure

Private gondola rides, skiing or hiking, gourmet picnic in meadows.

Day 4 – Pahalgam & Lidder Valley

Luxury stays in scenic resorts, private excursions, horseback rides.

Day 5 – Sonmarg & Yusmarg

Private day trips to glaciers and meadows, fine dining, sunset views.

Day 6 – Gurez Valley

Private guided tour of villages, authentic local experiences.

Day 7 – Leisure & Departure

Relaxed morning, airport transfer, end of luxury journey.
