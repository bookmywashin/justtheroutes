Adventure Kashmir

Thrilling treks, skiing, and exploration in Kashmir’s wild landscapes.

Itinerary Overview

Day 1 – Srinagar Arrival

Check-in at houseboat, evening Shikara ride.

Day 2 – Gulmarg Skiing / Adventure

Gondola ride, skiing, snowboarding, or hiking in meadows.

Day 3 – Pahalgam Trek

Moderate trek in Lidder Valley, scenic photography, riverside picnic.

Day 4 – Sonmarg Glacier Trek

Explore glaciers and streams, short trek for adventure enthusiasts.

Day 5 – Yusmarg Exploration

Trekking through meadows, forest trails, evening return to Srinagar.

Day 6 – Departure

Morning leisure, airport transfer.
