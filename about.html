About JustTheRoutes

JustTheRoutes was born in the heart of Kashmir — not in a boardroom, but in the valleys, streets, and mountains we call home. Built by locals who have grown up listening to the stories of Dal Lake, walking through pine forests, and witnessing Kashmir through every season, we understand this land beyond guidebooks and maps.

For us, travel is not about ticking destinations off a list. It’s about slowing down, connecting with people, and experiencing a place as it truly is. That philosophy lies at the core of everything we do.

Over the years, we noticed that many travelers came to Kashmir with uncertainty — questions about safety, comfort, authenticity, and value. JustTheRoutes was created to answer those concerns with honesty, transparency, and genuine local care.

We work closely with trusted drivers, verified hotels, and local partners to ensure every journey is safe, comfortable, and responsibly planned. Our itineraries are designed to respect local culture, protect natural beauty, and provide travelers with meaningful experiences.

Whether it’s a couple on their honeymoon, a family creating lifelong memories, or a solo traveler seeking peace, we treat every guest as our own. Because when you travel with us, you’re not just a customer — you’re a guest in our home.

JustTheRoutes is more than a travel company. It is our way of sharing the Kashmir we love — responsibly, safely, and wholeheartedly.
