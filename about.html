Born in Kashmir. Built by Locals.

Your Gateway to Timeless Journeys

JustTheRoutes was born in the heart of Kashmir — shaped not by trends, but by lived experiences. We grew up among these valleys, walked these trails, and listened to the stories that flow through Dal Lake and beyond.

Over time, we saw travelers arrive with curiosity and leave with memories, but also with unanswered questions — about safety, authenticity, and trust. JustTheRoutes was created to change that.

We believe travel should feel personal. Every route we design is backed by local knowledge, verified partners, and a deep respect for Kashmir’s people and environment.

Whether it’s a honeymoon, a family vacation, or a luxury escape, we treat every traveler as a guest in our home — because that’s how Kashmir has always welcomed the world.
