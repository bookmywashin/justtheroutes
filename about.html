Our Story

JustTheRoutes was created with a simple belief — Kashmir should be experienced through the eyes of those who truly call it home.

We are not a generic travel company or a distant booking platform. We are locals, born and raised among these mountains, valleys, and lakes. Every route we design comes from lived experience, not brochures.

More Than a Destination

For us, Kashmir is not a checklist of attractions. It is home. Every winding road, hidden meadow, and sunrise over Dal Lake carries stories we’ve grown up with.

JustTheRoutes exists to offer journeys that feel unhurried, personal, and deeply authentic — experiences that stay with you long after you return.

Why We Do What We Do

Traveling to Kashmir often comes with questions — safety, trust, transparency, and authenticity. As locals, we’ve seen travelers overwhelmed by rushed itineraries and impersonal packages.

Our goal is to change that. We design journeys that respect your pace, balance comfort with discovery, and offer complete clarity at every step.

Local Expertise. Real Experiences.

Being based in Srinagar gives us an unmatched advantage. We work directly with verified hotels, trusted drivers, and experienced local guides — many of whom we’ve known personally for years.

This ensures fair pricing, consistent quality, and honest promises. No middlemen. No hidden costs. What you see is exactly what you experience.

Responsible & Safe Tourism

Kashmir’s beauty is timeless — and fragile. We believe tourism should uplift local communities while preserving nature and culture.

Safety is central to everything we do. From route planning to accommodation selection, every detail is chosen with your comfort and peace of mind in focus.

Our Promise

When you travel with JustTheRoutes, you are not just booking a tour — you are placing your trust in people who genuinely care.
