3 Nights / 4 Days Kashmir Tour

Quick but immersive exploration of Kashmir’s highlights.

Itinerary Overview

Day 1 – Arrival in Srinagar

Arrival at Srinagar airport, transfer to Dal Lake houseboat. Evening Shikara ride to enjoy the sunset and local life.

Day 2 – Gulmarg

Drive to Gulmarg, enjoy Gondola ride with panoramic views. Optional skiing or meadow walk. Evening return to Srinagar.

Day 3 – Pahalgam

Visit Lidder River and Baisaran meadows. Enjoy horse rides and riverside walks. Evening return to Srinagar for local shopping or leisure.

Day 4 – Departure

Leisure morning, breakfast, airport transfer, end of tour.
