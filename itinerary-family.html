Family Tour

Fun, safe and memorable Kashmir experiences for the whole family.

Itinerary Overview

Day 1 – Srinagar Arrival

Check-in at houseboat, short Shikara ride, settle in.

Day 2 – Srinagar Sightseeing

Mughal Gardens, local markets, and Dal Lake exploration with kids.

Day 3 – Gulmarg

Meadow walks, Gondola ride, family-friendly snow activities.

Day 4 – Pahalgam

Lidder River picnic, Baisaran meadow, pony rides, forest walks.

Day 5 – Sonmarg

Glacier sightseeing, short treks, optional photography.

Day 6 – Departure

Morning at leisure, airport drop-off.
